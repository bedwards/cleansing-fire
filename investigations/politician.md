# Investigation Template: Elected Official

Investigate an elected official's funding sources, legislative record, lobbying connections, and accountability to constituents vs donors.

## Target Official
[OFFICIAL_NAME] — [OFFICE] — [STATE/DISTRICT]

## Phase 1: Follow the Money (plugins/civic-fec)
- Top 20 donors (individuals and organizations)
- Industry breakdown of contributions
- PAC contributions received
- Self-funding amounts
- Fundraising trajectory over time
- Out-of-district vs in-district funding ratio
- Comparison to opponents' funding

## Phase 2: Legislative Record (plugins/civic-legiscan)
- Bills sponsored and co-sponsored
- Voting record on key issues
- Committee assignments and attendance
- Bills introduced that match donor interests
- Amendments offered
- Floor statements and their alignment with donor priorities

## Phase 3: Lobbying Connections (plugins/lobby-tracker)
- Lobbyists who donated to this official
- Meetings with registered lobbyists
- Revolving door: former staff now lobbying, lobbyists now on staff
- Industry lobbying spending targeting this official's committees

## Phase 4: Cross-Reference (plugins/civic-crossref)
- Correlation between donations received and votes cast
- Timeline: donation → meeting → vote → policy outcome
- Which industries get return on investment?
- Comparison to peer officials (same party, same state)

## Phase 5: News and Public Record (plugins/news-monitor)
- Investigative reporting on this official
- Ethics complaints or investigations
- Public statements vs voting record
- Campaign promises vs legislative record
- Social media analysis: who do they engage with?

## Phase 6: Constituent Impact
- How do their votes affect their district?
- Economic data for their constituents
- Are they representing donors or voters?
- Policy outcomes for their top-funded industries vs constituent needs

## Phase 7: Synthesis
- Accountability score: donors vs constituents
- Key findings
- Conflicts of interest identified
- Comparison to peers
- Public interest implications
- Recommended follow-up

## Output Format
1. Executive summary
2. Official profile (office, party, tenure, committees)
3. Funding analysis with visualizations
4. Legislative record analysis
5. Lobbying connection map
6. Donor-to-vote correlation analysis
7. Sources and methodology
8. Recommended investigations
