# Investigation Template: Government Contract

Investigate a specific government contract for waste, fraud, abuse, or conflicts of interest.

## Target Contract
[CONTRACT_ID_OR_DESCRIPTION] — [AGENCY] — [CONTRACTOR]

## Phase 1: Contract Details (plugins/civic-spending)
- Contract value (initial and modifications)
- Awarding agency and office
- Contract type (fixed-price, cost-plus, T&M)
- Competition: sole-source vs competitive bid
- Period of performance
- Place of performance
- NAICS code and product/service code
- Subcontracting plan

## Phase 2: Contractor Profile (plugins/corp-sec)
- Company size and revenue
- Ownership structure
- Government contracts as % of revenue
- Other government contracts held
- Past performance ratings
- Debarment or suspension history
- SEC filings if publicly traded

## Phase 3: Political Connections
### Campaign Finance (plugins/civic-fec)
- Contractor PAC donations to oversight committee members
- Executive personal donations
- Donations to officials who influence contract decisions
- Timeline: donations vs contract awards

### Lobbying (plugins/lobby-tracker)
- Lobbying spending by the contractor
- Specific issues lobbied
- Lobbyists employed (especially former government officials)
- Contacts with contracting officers or decision-makers

## Phase 4: Cross-Reference (plugins/civic-crossref)
- Other contracts awarded to same contractor by same agency
- Pattern analysis: does this contractor consistently win?
- Comparison to similar contracts at other agencies
- Cost analysis: is this contract priced appropriately?
- Modification history: did the contract grow significantly?

## Phase 5: Oversight and News (plugins/news-monitor)
- GAO or IG reports mentioning this contract or contractor
- Congressional hearing testimony
- Investigative journalism
- FOIA requests filed about this contract
- Protest history (GAO or Court of Federal Claims)

## Phase 6: Synthesis
- Is this contract good value for taxpayers?
- Conflicts of interest identified
- Competition concerns
- Cost growth analysis
- Performance assessment
- Political influence on contract award
- Recommended follow-up (FOIA, IG complaint, congressional referral)

## Output Format
1. Executive summary
2. Contract profile
3. Contractor profile
4. Political influence analysis
5. Cost-benefit assessment
6. Red flags identified
7. Comparison to similar contracts
8. Sources and methodology
9. Recommended actions
